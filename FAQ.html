Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why is Medlife changing into PharmEasy?
Medlife was recently acquired by PharmEasy, and we are merging the two brands in order to be better and more efficient at what we do. We believe that the skills of both these teams, combined, will generate quality, ease and better service.
2. What does this mean for Medlife Customers?
All Medlife customers are now automatically registered with Pharmeasy. Customers just need to login to the PharmEasy app to start using their medlife account there. This is done using the same phone number as on Medlife. All your digitized prescriptions and images dating back to a year will be available on the Pharmeasy platform.
3. What benefits do I get from moving my account to PharmEasy?
The Medlife App & the Medlife website will stop taking orders from 25th May 2021 onwards. For a smooth transition, an exclusive offer of Flat 30% OFF on First 3 Medicine Orders and Extra 20% OFF on 1st Lab Test will be available on purchases made on Pharmeasy. This offer is only applicable for users who registered on Medlife before 25th of May.
4. What happens to my current order history?
Users can still access their old data on Medlife App till 31st Dec 2021, but they will not be able to place any orders. Post 31st Dec 2021 this data will not be available on Medlife anymore.
5. Why am I not able to register / sign up on Medlife?
Medlife is not accepting any new registrations (from 25th May). In order to make a purchase please download the PharmEasy app.
6. How do I transfer my Medlife account to PharmEasy?
Login to the PharmEasy app using the Medlife registered phone number and your details will be automatically visible there.
7. What happens to my subscriptions?
Your Medlife Subscription will be transferred to Pharmeasy. You’ll get a FREE ‘Pharmeasy Plus’ Subscription for 6 months which will give you the following benefits: Extra 5% cashback on Medicines and Healthcare Products
FREE Delivery on all orders above Rs. 200
